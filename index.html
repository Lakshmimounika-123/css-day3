Engineers day

Engineers' Day is celebrated on September 15 in India to commemorate the birth anniversary of Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, a renowned Indian engineer and statesman. He was known as the "Father of Modern India" for his contributions to the development of the country's infrastructure.
